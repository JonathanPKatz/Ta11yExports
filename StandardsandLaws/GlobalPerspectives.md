<h1 id="global-perspectives">Global Perspectives</h1>
<h2 id="summary">Summary</h2>
<p>Disability advocacy promotes and protects the rights of people with disabilities. Advocacy can improve the quality of life for people with disabilities. It can also provide a sense of strength and community.</p>
<p>There are a growing number of countries with laws that protect these rights. Disability laws address access to the physical world. More and more, they also address access to digital content. Together, they can help people with disabilities fully participate in the modern world.</p>
