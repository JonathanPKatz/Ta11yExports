<h1 id="site-navigation">Site Navigation</h1>
<h2 id="summary">Summary</h2>
<p>Providing clear, consistent navigation makes it easier to use a website. It especially helps people with disabilities. Navigation includes menus, breadcrumbs, search, site maps, and more:</p>
<ul>
<li>Consistent navigation makes it easy to understand a site’s structure. It helps people learn to use a site.</li>
<li>Descriptive navigation reveals a site’s content. It helps people find needed content.</li>
<li>Navigation options offer alternatives. Someone who has a hard time using dropdown menus may find searching for content easy.</li>
<li>Current location indicators tell people where they are within a website. It helps to keep people from getting lost, especially on a large website.</li>
</ul>
