<h1 id="creating-a-disability-inclusion-plan">Creating a Disability Inclusion Plan</h1>
<h2 id="summary">Summary</h2>
<p>Each step of becoming disability friendly includes a lot of details, and it is easy to become overwhelmed. It may help to think of it as a journey. On any journey, it is important to understand your current status and your next steps.</p>
